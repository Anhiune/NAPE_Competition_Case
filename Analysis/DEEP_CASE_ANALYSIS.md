## 3. PJM MARKET DEEP DIVE

### What PJM Is and Why It's the Battleground

PJM Interconnection is the world's largest competitive wholesale electricity market:
- **Coverage:** 13 states + DC, 65 million people
- **Capacity:** ~180 GW of installed generation
- **Northern Virginia:** The U.S. epicenter of data center development ("Data Center Alley")
- **Market type:** Competitive ‚Äî generators must bid and win to dispatch

### Revenue Stream 1: Energy Market (LMP)

| Period | Average PJM LMP |
|--------|----------------|
| H1 2024 | $31.70/MWh |
| H1 2025 | **$51.75/MWh** (+63%) |
| Case assumption | $55‚Äì60/MWh |

üìê **Why LMP rose 63% in one year:**
```
LMP is set by the marginal (last) generator.
As demand rises (data centers) and supply falls (plant retirements):
‚Üí More expensive generators set the price more often
‚Üí Average LMP rises

Data centers added ~3,000 MW of new peak demand in PJM in 2025 alone.
12.3 GW of capacity retired in 2025 (mostly coal/gas).
Net supply tightening: demand up + supply down = price up.
```

### Revenue Stream 2: Capacity Market (BRA Auction)

| Delivery Year | Capacity Price (most zones) | Dominion Zone (NoVA) |
|---|---|---|
| 2024/2025 | $28.92/MW-day | ~$50/MW-day |
| 2025/2026 | **$269.92/MW-day** (+833%) | **$444.26/MW-day** |
| 2026/2027 | **$329.17/MW-day** (record) | Similar premium |

üìê **Annual capacity revenue for an 800 MW nuclear plant:**
```
At $329.17/MW-day (2026/2027 rate):
Annual revenue = 800 MW √ó $329.17/MW-day √ó 365 days
= 800 √ó $329.17 √ó 365
= $96.2M/year in capacity revenue alone

At Dominion Zone rate ($444/MW-day):
= 800 √ó $444 √ó 365 = $129.8M/year
```

üìê **Why capacity prices rose 833% in one year:**
```
Data centers responsible for 63% of the price increase (IEEFA).
Total cost to customers: $9.3B additional in 2025/2026.
Mechanism: More demand + fewer plants = tighter supply/demand balance
‚Üí Generators can bid higher and still clear the auction.
```

### The Interconnection Crisis ‚Äî A Critical Deal-Breaker Fact

- PJM's interconnection queue has **170,000+ MW of new generation requests** (as of Jan 2026)
- Only **57 GW** have successfully completed the study process and can proceed to construction
- New projects face **1‚Äì2 year review timelines** just to get into the queue
- PJM received a **D- grade** from Advanced Energy United for interconnection efficiency (2024)
- Dominion Zone data center load forecast: **20 GW by 2037** (up from 5.7 GW in 2022 forecast)

**Why this matters for the case:**
- **Build (new CCGT):** Must join the interconnection queue. 1‚Äì2 year review + 24-month construction = **3‚Äì4 years to first power.** This is a massive disadvantage.
- **Buy (existing):** Already interconnected. No queue. **Immediate advantage.**
- **Partner (co-location):** Behind-the-meter bypasses the interconnection queue entirely. **Biggest advantage.**
